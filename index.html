About Us

Mahaveer NDT Services is a leading provider of Non-Destructive Testing services based in Kota, Rajasthan. We specialize in Radiography, Ultrasonic Testing, Dye Penetrant Testing, and Magnetic Particle Testing, ensuring the highest standards of safety and quality for industrial projects.

Our Services

Radiography Testing (RT)

High-precision radiography services ensuring defect-free structures and welds.

Ultrasonic Testing (UT)

Accurate detection of internal flaws using advanced ultrasonic techniques.

Dye Penetrant Testing (DPT)

Efficient surface crack detection for critical structural components.

Magnetic Particle Testing (MPT)

Quick identification of surface and near-surface defects in ferromagnetic materials.

Concrete Testing

Accurate assessment of concrete integrity for safe infrastructure projects.
